Welcome to the

Health Information Platform(HIP) for the WHO South-East Asia Region.

HIP allows users to access an integrated database of the indicators, use interactive analytics tools to generate tables and charts, view maps and monitoring dashboards, and generate visualizations that contain health information from WHO global health estimates and nationally reported values accross the 11 member states of the WHO South-East Asia Region.The HIP is poweres by the District Health Information Software v.2(DHIS2),which is used by 9 out of 11 countries.

Click On the HIP toolboxes below to explore health indicators in the 11 Countries in South-East Asia Region.

Generate Tables

Create Data tables on single or Multiple indicators,countries and years.


Generate Charts

Visualize data in line graph,scatter plots, bar, pie and diamond charts.


Render Maps

Plot available indicators on the map for specific period to compare data across the region.


View Dashboard

Visulize data and indicators by health themes, including the health SDGs and Universal Health Coverage(UHC)
